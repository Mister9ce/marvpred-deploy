Answers to Common Inquiries About MARVPred

MARVpred is a machine learning-powered tool designed to predict chemical compound inhibition against Marburg virus. It analyzes chemical structures using SMILES notation to identify potential antiviral compounds that could help in the fight against this deadly hemorrhagic fever virus.
Simply navigate to our Predict page and enter the SMILES notation of your chemical compound. Our advanced machine learning model will analyze the structure and provide predictions about its potential inhibitory activity against Marburg virus.
SMILES (Simplified Molecular Input Line Entry System) is a chemical notation that allows you to represent molecular structures as text strings. For example, water (Hâ‚‚O) is represented as "O" and ethanol as "CCO". This format enables our AI to understand and analyze chemical structures.
Our machine learning model has been trained on extensive datasets of chemical compounds and their antiviral activities. While predictions provide valuable insights for research, they should be validated through experimental studies. The tool is designed to accelerate drug discovery research, not replace laboratory testing.
MARVpred is developed for research purposes to advance the fight against Marburg virus. For commercial applications or large-scale research projects, please contact our team to discuss licensing and collaboration opportunities.
